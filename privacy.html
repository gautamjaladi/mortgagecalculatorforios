Privacy Policy

App: Home Metric (“the App”)
Developer: Gautam Jaladi (“we”, “us”, or “our”)

Overview (plain English)

  • Your mortgage inputs and saved properties are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.
  • If you allow location access, we use it to show local weather, regional benchmarks, and location-based context.
  • You can use the App without granting location or notification permissions, but some features may be limited.
  • You can delete your saved data in the App or by uninstalling the App.

1) Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the App and provide its features.

  • Information you enter: This may include property details, home price, taxes, HOA, insurance, maintenance assumptions, income assumptions, debt assumptions, down payment, loan term, interest rate, PMI assumptions, notes, and other calculator inputs you choose to provide.
  • Saved scenarios and notes: If you save properties, scenarios, or notes in the App, that information is stored locally on your device.
  • Location information (optional): If you grant location permission, the App may access your approximate or precise location while you are using the App.
  • Notification preferences (optional): If you allow notifications, the App may store your notification settings on your device.
  • Basic technical/request data: When the App connects to third-party or public data sources, those services may receive basic internet request information such as IP address, device type, operating system, and user agent.

2) How we collect information

  • We collect information you manually enter into the App.
  • We collect location data only if you grant permission through iOS.
  • We collect limited technical/request data automatically when the App makes network requests to fetch data.
  • If we add analytics or crash reporting in the future, we will update this policy and related App Store privacy disclosures before enabling those tools.

3) How we use information

We use information to:

  • perform mortgage and affordability calculations;
  • display charts, summaries, insights, and saved scenarios;
  • generate exported reports or PDFs;
  • show local weather, area benchmarks, and region-based insights;
  • send reminders or alerts that you choose to enable;
  • respond to support requests and improve the App.

4) Location information

Location access is optional. If you allow it, we may use your approximate or precise location while the App is in use to provide local weather, regional housing or economic context, benchmark data, or to simplify location-based inputs.

We do not use your location for advertising or cross-app tracking. You can revoke location access at any time in your iPhone settings.

5) Third-party services and public data sources

The App may fetch data from third-party services or public sources to support App features. Depending on the feature you use, these services may receive limited request information needed to return results.

Examples may include:

  • weather providers;
  • public economic or housing datasets;
  • public benchmark or regional data providers;
  • external news or content feeds.

These third parties operate under their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services or websites.

6) In-app purchases and subscriptions

If the App offers paid upgrades, subscriptions, or other in-app purchases, payment transactions are processed by Apple through your App Store account. We do not receive your full payment card details.

7) Data retention

Your saved property, budget, and loan information remains on your device until you delete it in the App or uninstall the App.

If you contact us by email, we may retain that correspondence for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to you, maintain support records, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

8) Your choices and controls

  • You can edit or delete saved scenarios and properties inside the App.
  • You can uninstall the App to remove locally stored App data from your device.
  • You can turn location and notification permissions on or off in iOS Settings.
  • You can email us if you have questions about your data or would like support-related information deleted, where applicable.

9) Security

We use reasonable safeguards and standard secure technologies, including HTTPS where applicable, to protect information handled by the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10) Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can review and delete it if appropriate.

11) International and regional privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws, including rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we may hold about you. To make a request, contact us at the email address listed below.
