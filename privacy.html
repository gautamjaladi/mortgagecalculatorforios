Privacy Policy

Developer: Gautam Jaladi (“we”, “us”)

Overview (plain English)

  • Your mortgage inputs and saved properties are stored on your device.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • If you allow location access, we use it to show local weather and regional benchmarks. Location may be sent to third-party services only to return those results.
  • You can use the App without granting location or notification permissions (some features may be limited).

1) Information you provide (stored on your device)

The App lets you enter and save information such as property details (address or nickname, price, taxes, HOA, insurance), loan assumptions (down payment, rate, term, PMI assumptions), and budget/income assumptions you choose to enter (income, debts, maintenance/utilities assumptions). This information is saved locally on your device to power calculations, insights, and PDF exports.

2) Location information (optional)

If you grant location permission (When In Use), we access your approximate or precise location while the App is in use to provide local weather and regional benchmarks/context (for example, area median income or area averages) based on your region.

How it’s used: the App may send your location (latitude/longitude) to third-party services to fetch weather and regional lookup results. We do not use location for advertising or tracking.

You can change location permissions anytime in iOS Settings. If you deny location access, you can still use the App, but local features may be unavailable or require manual input.

3) Notifications (optional)

If you enable notifications, the App may send notifications you opt into (for example, reminders or alerts you configure). You can turn notifications off anytime in iOS Settings.

4) Network requests and third-party services

The App fetches certain information from third-party or public data sources to provide features like weather, benchmarks, and headlines. Depending on what you use, requests may include your approximate location/coordinates (if you allow location), a region identifier (ZIP/city/state) derived from your location or entered by you, or general queries to fetch public headlines.

Third-party services may receive standard request information (such as IP address and user agent) as part of normal internet communication. Their handling of that information is governed by their own privacy policies.

Examples of sources may include (edit to match your actual sources): Open-Meteo, U.S. Census, FRED, FCC, FFIEC/HMDA public endpoints, Google News RSS.

5) Analytics and tracking

We do not use advertising tracking in the App. If we add analytics or crash reporting tools in the future, we will update this policy and App Store privacy disclosures accordingly.

6) Reports and exports (PDF)

You may export a PDF report from the App. Exported reports may include the property/loan inputs and insights shown in the App for the selected property. When you share or save a report, it is handled by the destination you choose (Files, Mail, Messages). You control where it goes.

7) Data retention and deletion

Your inputs and saved properties remain on your device until you delete them in the App or uninstall the App. The App may provide an option to delete saved properties/scenarios in Settings.

8) Security

We use standard iOS protections and secure network connections (HTTPS) when retrieving data. No security method is perfect, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

9) Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the effective date and may notify you in the App if changes are material.
